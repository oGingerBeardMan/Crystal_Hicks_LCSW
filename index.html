EMDR Therapy

Healing that reaches beyond talking about the past.

EMDR (Eye Movement Desensitization and Reprocessing) is an evidence-based therapy that helps the brain process distressing memories so they no longer carry the same emotional intensity.

Many clients find that EMDR allows them to move forward with greater peace, confidence, and emotional freedom.

  • Evidence-based trauma treatment
  • Integrated into weekly sessions or intensives
  • Personalized for your goals and readiness
  • Focused on lasting healing, not just symptom relief
About Crystal

Healing begins with feeling safe, understood, and supported.

I'm Crystal Hicks, LCSW, and I believe therapy should be a place where you can exhale. A place where you don't have to carry everything alone.

My passion is helping women navigate trauma, anxiety, burnout, grief, infertility, caregiver stress, and life's major transitions with compassion, curiosity, and evidence-based care.

I integrate EMDR and other trauma-informed approaches to help women move beyond simply managing symptoms and toward lasting healing. Every treatment plan is personalized to your goals, your pace, and your unique story.

If faith is an important part of your life, it can be thoughtfully incorporated into the therapeutic process. If it isn't, you'll receive the same compassionate, personalized care in a welcoming environment that honors your values.

Whether we're meeting for weekly sessions, extended appointments, or a therapy intensive, my hope is that you'll leave each session feeling lighter, more grounded, and better equipped for whatever lies ahead.

Frequently Asked Questions

Common Questions

Do I have to be Christian to work with you? +

No. My practice welcomes women from all backgrounds. Faith integration is always optional and only included if it aligns with your goals and preferences.

How do I know if an intensive is right for me? +

During your consultation we'll discuss your goals, history, schedule, and determine together whether weekly sessions or an intensive experience is the best fit.

Are telehealth appointments available? +

Yes. Secure telehealth appointments are available throughout the state of Florida for clients who prefer remote therapy.

Do you accept insurance? +

This practice is self-pay, allowing therapy to remain personalized, private, and focused entirely on your treatment goals rather than insurance requirements.
